    Incident reporting task

    You have seen on Blackboard that an incident occurred whilst supporting Victoria with injection techniques. Before you were able to stop her, Victoria re-sheathed the needle and caught her thumb with the needle.

    You now need to follow policy and complete an incident report and then an Incidents in Practice (IP) form.

    What to do

    Complete the incident form using a clear, factual account of what happened. The role, incident time, discovered time and location have already been set for this activity.

    When the form is complete, click Review summary. Check the summary carefully, then click Submit report.

    After submitting the incident report, use the button shown to continue to the Incidents in Practice (IP) form.

    More detail required

    Incident reports need enough clear, factual detail for the reviewer to understand what happened, what action was taken, and whether the area is now safe.
